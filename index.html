About Me

Hello! I am a PhD scholar in Computational Biology at IIIT-Delhi.

My work lies at the exciting interface between RNA biology, Big Data, and Artificial Intelligence. I am passionate about building computational tools, advocating for open science, and connecting molecules to biological mechanisms using machine learning.
