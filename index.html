01 / About

Hello, World.

I'm a PhD scholar in Computational Biology at IIIT-Delhi, working at the intersection of RNA biology, machine learning, and large-scale biological data.

My research focuses on building predictive computational tools for understanding non-coding RNA subcellular localization, antimicrobial peptides, and therapeutic target identification — connecting molecular biology to actionable bioinformatic insights.

I'm passionate about open science, reproducible research, and lowering the barrier to computational biology for wet-lab researchers through accessible, well-documented tools.
